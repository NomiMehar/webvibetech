Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our website speed optimization services

How long does website speed optimization take?

The timeline for website speed optimization varies based on website size and complexity. A simple website optimization typically takes 1-2 weeks, while complex websites with many pages may take 3-4 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the initial performance audit.

What improvements can I expect from speed optimization?

Our speed optimization services typically achieve 50-80% reduction in page load time, 60-90% reduction in page size, improved Google PageSpeed scores (90+ on mobile and desktop), better Core Web Vitals scores, reduced bounce rates, and improved conversions. Results vary based on your website's current state.

Can you optimize my existing website?

Absolutely! We provide speed optimization services for existing websites. We analyze your current performance, identify bottlenecks, and implement comprehensive optimizations. We work with websites built on any platform including WordPress, custom websites, e-commerce platforms, and more.

Do you provide ongoing performance monitoring?

Yes, we provide ongoing performance monitoring to ensure your website maintains optimal speed. We track performance metrics, identify new issues, and implement continuous improvements. Regular monitoring helps prevent performance degradation over time and ensures your website stays fast.

What is the cost of website speed optimization?

The cost of website speed optimization depends on various factors including website size, complexity, current performance, and optimization requirements. Basic optimization starts from $99, while comprehensive optimization for large websites can cost more. We provide detailed quotes after the initial performance audit. Contact us for a free consultation and custom quote.

What is included in your speed optimization services?

Our speed optimization services include performance audit, image optimization, code minification, caching implementation, CDN integration, database optimization, lazy loading, Core Web Vitals optimization, and performance monitoring. Additional services like ongoing maintenance and advanced optimizations can be added based on your requirements.
