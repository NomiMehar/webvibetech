Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our UI/UX design services

How long does a UI/UX design project take?

The timeline for UI/UX design projects varies based on project scope and complexity. A simple website design typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex applications with multiple screens may take 6-10 weeks or more. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide Figma to HTML conversion services?

Yes, we provide Figma to HTML conversion services. We convert your Figma design files into pixel-perfect, responsive websites that match your design exactly. We also provide Adobe XD to HTML conversion services. Our conversion services ensure clean, semantic code and optimal performance.

Can you redesign my existing website or app?

Absolutely! We provide website redesign and app redesign services. We conduct design audits to identify usability issues and areas for improvement, then create improved designs that enhance user experience and drive better results. Our redesign services focus on modernizing your interface while maintaining your brand identity.

What design tools do you use?

We use industry-leading design tools including Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, InVision, and Adobe Creative Suite. We choose the best tools based on your project requirements and team collaboration needs. We're proficient in all major design tools and can work with your preferred design software.

What is the cost of UI/UX design services?

The cost of UI/UX design services depends on various factors including project scope, number of screens, complexity, design requirements, and timeline. Simple website designs start from $99, while complex application designs can cost more. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and custom quote.

What is included in your UI/UX design services?

Our UI/UX design services include user research, wireframing, prototyping, visual design, user experience design, responsive design, design systems, usability testing, and design handoff. We also provide Figma to HTML conversion services. Additional services like design audits, redesigns, and ongoing design support can be added based on your requirements.
