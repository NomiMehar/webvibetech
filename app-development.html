Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our mobile app development services

How long does a mobile app development project take?

The timeline for mobile app development varies based on project scope and complexity. A simple mobile app typically takes 6-10 weeks, while complex apps with advanced features may take 12-20 weeks or more. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Do you build native or cross-platform apps?

We build both native and cross-platform apps. Native apps (iOS with Swift/Objective-C, Android with Kotlin/Java) offer optimal performance and platform-specific features. Cross-platform apps (React Native, Flutter) allow code sharing between iOS and Android, reducing development time and cost. We recommend the best approach based on your requirements.

Can you help with an existing app?

Absolutely! We provide app maintenance, updates, and enhancement services. Whether you need to update your existing app, add new features, fix bugs, improve performance, or migrate to a new platform, we can help. We also offer app redesign services to modernize your app's UI/UX.

Do you handle app store and play store submission?

Yes, we handle complete app store submission for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store. This includes preparing app store listings, screenshots, descriptions, keywords, compliance checks, and submission process. We also provide App Store Optimization (ASO) services to improve your app's visibility and downloads.

What is the cost of mobile app development?

The cost of mobile app development depends on various factors including app complexity, features, platform (iOS, Android, or both), design requirements, and timeline. Simple apps start from $1,999, while complex enterprise apps can cost more. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and custom quote.

What is included in your app development services?

Our app development services include UI/UX design, native or cross-platform development, backend integration, API development, testing across devices, app store submission, initial support, and documentation. Additional features like push notifications, analytics, payment integration, and social media integration can be added based on your requirements.
